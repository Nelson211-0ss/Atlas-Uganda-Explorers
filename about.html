Who We Are

Atlas Uganda Explorers

Atlas Uganda Explorers is a trusted full-service tour and travel company based in Jinja City, Uganda, with nearly a decade of experience delivering exceptional travel experiences across Uganda and the East African region.

Established with a passion for showcasing Uganda's rich cultural heritage, natural beauty, and vibrant communities, we have grown into a reputable brand recognised by both local and international travellers.

We specialise in tailor-made travel solutions for individuals, families, couples, corporate groups, and organisations, ensuring every journey is seamless, memorable, and uniquely curated to suit our clients' needs and interests.
